# Video

Responsive embedded video as a filmstrip in 16:9 aspect ratio. Supports YouTube, Vimeo, DailyMotion (and possibly others)

<Props of={Video} />

## Basic usage

### Youtube

<Playground>
  <Video
    src="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U3ucpVlaeK8"
    title="Hoàng Thùy Linh - Bánh Trôi Nước (Woman)"
  />
</Playground>

### Vimeo

<Playground>
  <Video src="https://vimeo.com/13628090" title="Poets of the Fall - Lift" />
</Playground>

### DailyMotion

<Playground>
